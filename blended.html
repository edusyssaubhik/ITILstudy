The ITILstudy Blended course is the most appropriate course for the students who cannot attend the 2-day ITIL Foundation classroom course.

Benefits of ITILstudy BLENDED:

  • Access to Study guides, Chapter test, Podcasts and APMG sample papers.
  • Course fee includes Exam Voucher for ITIL Foundation Certification exam at any APMG Certified Test Center.
  • Access to ITILstudy ITIL Foundation Simulated Test.

Course Description: ITILstudy Blended course comprises of ITIL Foundation Online course (E- learning Course) and exam voucher for ITIL Foundation exam.

ITIL Foundation Online course A student who enrolls for the ITILstudy BLENDED course will get access to the ITILstudy Foundation online course for . The students can allocate days of study depending upon their individual aptitude, skill sets, experience, and levels of comprehension.

ITIL Foundation Certification Exam Voucher ITILstudy will issue exam vouchers to all the students who enrol for the course. The students can give the exam at any available location within of getting the voucher.
